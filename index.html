Europe Trip 2025

June 27 – July 11 | Berlin • Munich • Wengen • Amsterdam & more

June 27 – Flight

We are flying from the US to Berlin, Germany(Cleveland -> Toronto -> Frankfurt -> Berlin). Arrive in the morning of the 28th.

June 28 – Berlin

Arrive, visit Berlin Wall Memorial, and free time.

June 29 – Berlin

Hop-on-hop-off city tour, afternoon Jewish Holocaust Museum, free evening.

June 30 – Munich

High speed train to Munich, Dachau concentration camp, free afternoon, and guided Bavarian food/market tour for dinner.

July 1 – Munich -> Zurich

Early moring to Neuschwanstein castle, travel to Schauffhausen for river cruise to Rhine falls, travel to Zurich.

July 2 – Baretswil, Lauterbrunnen, Lucerne, Wengen

Travel to Baretswil, hike to Tauferhohle, travel to Lauterbrunnen, Lunch in Lucerne, cogwheel train to Wengen, free evening.

July 3 – Wengen

Free day.

July 4 – Wengen

Free day.

July 5 – Wengen -> Bern

Visit Wenger homestead, Rothenbach im Emmental, Schallenberg Pass, travel to Trub, visit Hinter Hutten, Trachselwald castle, and travel to Bern.

July 6 – Bern -> Strasbourg

Sightseeing in Bern: Bear Pit, Federal Palace, Medieval Old Town, Parade of painted figures. Travel through French Riesling country to Strasbourg with lunch in Riquewhir.

July 7 – Strasbourg -> Mainz

Travel to Schafbusch, visit Hege Farm, church in Geisberg, travel to Gasthof zur Rose in Weinheim for lunch, visit Heligkreuz church, free afternoon in Heidelberg, and then travel to Mainz.

July 8 – Mainz

Travel to St Goar for Rhine River Cruise, return to Mainz after lunch, and free afternoon and evening in Mainz.

July 9 – Amsterdam

Travel to Frankfurt for flight to Amsterdam, check in to hotel and free afternoon, Anne Frank House at 7pm.

July 10 – Amsterdam

City tour of the Royal Palace, and a canal tour. afternoon train to Enkhuizen for Zuiderzee and tulip fields.

July 11 – Flight Home

Flight home: Amsterdam -> Toronto -> Cleveland.
